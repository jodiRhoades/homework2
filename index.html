About

Welcome to my website. You're looking at the very first attempt EVER of me being a web developer. For 24 years I've been doing massage therapy. I love what I do, but it has taken a toll on my body. I laugh now, because in the short 2 weeks learning about web development it has taken a toll on my mind!! (Just kidding...sorta) As a massage therapist, I have to problem solve my clients issues and have a creative approach that comes depending on the guests needs. I'm also detail and rule oreinted as an idividual. As I looked at software development, I saw the similarities with what I do in massage and with software development. As I learn more from bootcaamp, this site will evolve and hopefully so will your impressions of my work.
